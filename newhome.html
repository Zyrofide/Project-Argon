About Project Argon


Our Goal

The goal of Project Argon is simple: we want to provide free downloads for pre-configured "virtual-harddrives" for the virtualization software "Virtualbox". Virtualbox is a powerful piece of software that allows you to "emulate" a computer, basically run a computer inside a computer. This can be useful if, for example, you want to try out another operating system, but you don't want to install it on your system.


How does it work?

So, you got here. You read what our goal is. But how does it work? Well, it's not that hard: you just download virtualbox here and install it. Once you've finished installing it, you can minimize Virtualbox. Then you download one of the images we provide, and you save it in your Downloads folder or somewhere else. Then you go to Virtualbox, and you choose "machine" in the Menubar. In that menu, you choose "add ", or you simply press "CTRL+A". Then you navigate to the location where you downloaded and extracted the virtual machine you downloaded from our site.
